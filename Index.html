Who We Are

Batch 2025

We are the Mechanical Engineering Batch of 2025 at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology — a tight-knit cohort of problem-solvers, designers, and future engineers who arrived at the doors of KUET with one shared ambition: to build things that matter.

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at KUET is one of Bangladesh's most distinguished ME programs, known for its rigorous curriculum spanning thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, machine design, manufacturing processes, and robotics. We are proud to be its newest batch.

From the first day in CAD labs to late nights in the workshop, every challenge has brought us closer. This is our story — and it's just getting started.

The Path

Four Years of Engineering

⚙️
Year 1 — Foundations

Mathematics, Physics, Engineering Drawing, Basic Mechanics. The bedrock upon which everything else is built. Learning to see the world as a system of forces and constraints.

🔥
Year 2 — Core Mechanics

Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Strength of Materials, Machine Elements. Where theory gets hot and the real engineering begins.

🏭
Year 3 — Design & Manufacture

Machine Design, Manufacturing Processes, Heat Transfer, Dynamics of Machinery. Turning concepts into blueprints and blueprints into reality.

🚀
Year 4 — Thesis & Beyond

Final Year Project, Industrial Attachment, Electives in Robotics, CAD/CAM, Energy Engineering. Ready to engineer the future.
